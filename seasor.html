SEASOR

mobile platform designed for people who study abroad

April - May, 2016
Concept design

Concept

SEASOR, an mobile platform designed for students who study abroad, aims to connect those students together, helping them get the best out of their experience.

Background

Based on personal experience as an international student, studying abroad means facing challenges, which triggers me to find solutions on some of existing problems.
