About

Twenty-three years ago, she landed on this planet. She said hello to a whale drinking at the seaside, had a bite of a green strawberry picked up from the ground.

She was born with curiosity.

In the morning, she collects dew in the bucket, and walks around looking for wild fruits. She starts to live with the habitants and animals on the island. They teach her how to build tents, catch fish, etc.
