Tacit

Ambient lighting clock

Feb, 2016 - June, 2016
# Product Design

Concept

Inspired by traditional hourglass, Tacit is a dynamic water lamp that subtly implies time by the movement of light while creating a relaxing mood in a dark environment such as sauna room, bathroom.

Background

As a girl who likes to turn off the lights while taking a shower, I reckon darkness as important as lightness for people. In modern society people are surrounded by lots of lights which is more or less overwhelming. In that sense, I focus on 'design for darkness' to give back the experience that people could have a rest, think or calm down while feeling the beauty of lights in dim light condition.
