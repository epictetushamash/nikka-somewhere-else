PrintPlus

3D printed DIY headphone

Oct , 2015 - Feb, 2016
Product Design

Concept

Print+ has developed a DIY kit that enables you to make your own headphone. By combining 3D printed parts with components from the print+ kit you can create a headphone that is truly yours.

I Print the frame

II Get Printplus assemble kit

III Assemble your headphones

Outcome

Started on Kickstarter, this project succeed pledge nearly three times over the goal in one month via online (cooperation with journalists) and offline (Dutch Design Week) marketing.

Internship

The six-month internship at form + function Design Studio focused on developing and researching in 3D-printed products. My responsibility includes project launching, exhibitions together with the team.
